Delicious Chicken Biryani

Description

Biryani is one of the most loved dish in Pakisani cuisine. It is rich blend of Basmati rice and chicken or meat. It i also served at the weddings and other functions.

Chicken biryani is my favourite one. This recipe is incomplete so don't follow it. I am just using it as a project. There are plenty of websites which can be helpful in preparing this delicious Pakistani cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Shan Biryani Masala
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Prepare the biryani masala and marinate the Chicken
  2. Cook the chicen curry. While it's cooking over low heat, prepare the rice. Put water in a pot and boil it.
  3. Then put the rice in that. Once they fully boil then drain the water and set aside.
  4. Put a layer of the rice in a pot.
  5. Then add Chicken in it. Put another layer of rice on top of Chicken layer.
  6. After that put a lemon inside.
  7. Allow staem to develop and lower the heat.
