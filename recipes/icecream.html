Mango Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is one of my most delcious dessert. It has different flavours. It's hard to chose one flavour as favourite. So I will just put receipe for the Mango ice cream without mentioning any favourite flavour

Ingredients

  1. Mangoes
  2. whipping cream
  3. sugar
  4. syrup
  5. spiced rum
  6. kosher salt

Steps

  1. Combine mangoes, cream, sugar and syrup in a blender. Blend for 30 seconds
  2. Move to airtight container, Stir in spice rum and salt. Refrigerate for few hours
  3. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and frezee. Transfer to the container again and freeze until firm
