Raita

Description

This is combination of mint and youghurt. It is essential to spice up your Pakisani cuisine. It can great to have it alongside the starters.

Ingredients

  • Youghurt
  • Fresh mint
  • Green chilli
  • Litel bit of water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Combine chilli, mint, water and youghurt in a grinder.
  2. Grind it into a paste
  3. Season with salt
  4. Serve with the starters to enjoy
